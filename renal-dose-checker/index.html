Enter the patient’s most recent eGFR (or CrCl if using Cockcroft–Gault). If left blank, all renal bands will be shown without a highlighted recommendation.

Type at least 2 letters to see matching medicines. Click a medicine or press Enter to open its renal dosing page.

Safety / scope
• This is a decision-support prototype, not a complete renal formulary.
• Doses are simplified summaries based mainly on: BNF Prescribing in Renal Impairment, drug monographs, SPS DOAC guidance, NICE CKS and example trust documents.
• DOACs are formally dosed by creatinine clearance (CrCl), not eGFR – use Cockcroft–Gault for final decisions.
• Always apply clinical judgement, consider AKI, age, frailty, drug interactions, and local renal advice.
