Medicines Optimisation Toolkit

Helper tools for safe prescribing in UK general practice.

For use by prescribers only. Always check BNF, local guidance and use clinical judgement.

This toolkit brings together small, focused calculators to support complex prescribing decisions in UK general practice.

Important: These tools are decision aids, not a substitute for clinical assessment, up-to-date guidance or specialist advice. Doses are approximate and may need adjustment for age, frailty, comorbidity and renal / hepatic impairment.

Switch & Stop Antidepressants Live

Plans proportional tapers and switches between common UK antidepressants using realistic BNF tablet strengths.

Open antidepressant tool

Hypertension Management Beta

NICE and BIHS-aligned decision aid for reviewing and optimising antihypertensive therapy.

Open hypertension assistant

Opioid Conversion & Breakthrough Dosing Beta

Convert between opioids via oral morphine equivalent dose (OMED) and suggest breakthrough and rescue dosing.

Open opioid conversion

Dependence-forming Medicines Tapering Planned

Planned module for tapering of benzodiazepines, Z-drugs, codeine/tramadol and other dependence-forming medicines.

Steroid Tapering Planned

Planned module for prednisolone and other steroid tapering, taking duration of use and adrenal suppression risk into account.

Renal Dose Checker Beta

Search for a drug, enter eGFR, and view renal dose adjustments based on BNF, SPS and Renal Drug Handbook summaries.

Open renal dose checker
